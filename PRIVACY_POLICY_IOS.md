# Privacy Policy for Infinite Match (iOS)

**Last updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

Infinite Match ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application on iOS devices.

**Important for Parents:** Our game is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages, including children under 13. We take special care to protect children's privacy and comply with all applicable laws regarding children's data.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly
- **Game Progress Data**: Level completion, scores, achievements, and game statistics
- **Account Information**: Optional player name or nickname (no real names required)
- **Game Center Data**: Game Center account information, achievements, and leaderboard participation (if connected)
- **Purchase Information**: In-app purchase history, transaction details, and App Store receipts
- **Communication Data**: Customer support messages and feedback
- **Social Features**: Optional friend connections and multiplayer participation

### Information We Collect Automatically
- **Device Information**: iPhone/iPad model, iOS version, unique device identifiers (IDFA), hardware specifications, and device capabilities
- **Performance Data**: Game performance metrics, crash reports, error logs, and technical diagnostics
- **Usage Analytics**: Game sessions, features used, time spent playing, level progression, and interaction patterns
- **Network Information**: IP address, general location data (country/region level only), network type, and connection quality
- **Game Statistics**: Scores, levels completed, achievements earned, and gameplay patterns
- **App Store Data**: Download information, purchase history, and app usage statistics

### Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may collect additional information:
- **Unity Analytics**: Game analytics, performance monitoring, and user behavior analysis
- **Unity Cloud Services**: Game data synchronization, cloud saves, and cross-device functionality
- **Game Center**: Social features, achievements, and leaderboards
- **Apple's App Store Connect**: App analytics, crash reports, and performance metrics
- **Firebase (Google)**: Analytics, crash reporting, and remote configuration
- **Google AdMob**: Advertising services (if applicable)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Provide Game Services**: Deliver and maintain game functionality, features, and content
- **Improve User Experience**: Analyze gameplay patterns to enhance game design and user experience
- **Technical Support**: Diagnose and resolve technical issues, crashes, and performance problems
- **Customer Support**: Respond to inquiries, provide assistance, and resolve user issues
- **Game Analytics**: Understand user behavior, popular features, and areas for improvement
- **Security**: Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and ensure game integrity
- **Legal Compliance**: Meet legal obligations and regulatory requirements
- **Communication**: Send important game updates, notifications, and promotional content (with consent)
- **Cross-Device Sync**: Synchronize your progress across multiple iOS devices
- **Game Center Features**: Provide achievements, leaderboards, and social gaming features

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

**Special Protection for Children Under 13:**

Our game is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages, including children under 13. We take special care to protect children's privacy and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

### What We Do NOT Collect from Children Under 13:
- Real names or personal identifiers
- Email addresses or phone numbers
- Photos, videos, or audio recordings
- Precise location data (GPS coordinates)
- Contact information or address
- Social media account information
- Biometric data
- Behavioral advertising data
- Game Center account information (unless parental consent is provided)

### What We May Collect from Children Under 13:
- Game progress and achievements (anonymized)
- Device information (for technical support)
- Game performance data (for improving the game)
- General location (country/region level only)
- Basic usage statistics (anonymized)

### Parental Controls and Rights:
- **Review Rights**: Parents can request to review their child's information
- **Modification Rights**: Parents can request to modify or correct information
- **Deletion Rights**: Parents can request to delete their child's information
- **Opt-Out Rights**: Parents can opt out of data collection for their child
- **No Marketing**: We do not use children's information for behavioral advertising
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not share children's information with third parties for marketing

### How to Contact Us About Your Child's Information:
If you are a parent and have concerns about your child's privacy or want to review, modify, or delete your child's information, please contact us at:
- **Email**: privacy@infinite.match

- **Response Time**: We will respond within 30 days
